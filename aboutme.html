About me

I'm Rachel, a 19-year-old Singaporean programmer and junior college graduate. I love creating, developing, and bringing ideas to life digitally.

My interest in code started back in 2014, when I was into video games. I was often curious about the mechanics behind the gameplay, and tried my hand at modding them, which led me to discover the exciting world of digital tech.

Since then, I've spent the past 8+ years exploring my passion in various areas of tech: developing websites, apps and games, video editing, graphic design and 3D modelling.

I am currently interning at Tinkertanker, a technology and education company in Singapore.
